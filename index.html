popular question

What applications can benefit from Blockchain technology?

From large scale applications such Digitial Voting, Digital ID's, Supply Chain Management down to Point of Sales systems. Applications like this and of a similar nature can greatly benefit from Blockchain.

Do I Need To Have Any Prior Knowledge of Blockchain Technology?

No, Blockute will handle all the Blockchain development aspects for you.

Will Blockute just handle the business logic or will frontend design also be included?

For now Blockute will be able to create a personalized backend service with an included basic frontend that can easily be manipulated/changed. Though we do plan to introduce custom front-end templates in the future.

Isn't Blockchain just for cryptocurrency like Bitcoin?

No, blockchain technology allows you to create applications, big or small with a much higher level of security and data integrity than existing solutions.
